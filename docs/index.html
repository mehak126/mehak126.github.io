Mehak Dhaliwal

Hello! I'm an incoming PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at UC Santa Barbara, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yao Qin.

I recently graduated with an MS in Computer Science, specialising in AI from UC San Diego, where I researched in Prof. Julian McAuley's lab. Previously, I worked as a Senior Engineer in the on-device AI division at Samsung R&D Institute. I obtained my B.Tech from IIT, Delhi, where I worked with Prof. Aaditeshwar Seth.

At present, I am spending the summer as an Applied Scientist Intern in the Translation team at Amazon Web Services.

I'm passionate about research in NLP (Natural Language Processing), FAccT (Fairness, Accountability and Transparency) in AI, AI for Social Good and their intersection. Feel free to reach out for collaborations/opportunities!
